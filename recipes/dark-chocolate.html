Homemade Dark Chocolate for Valentine's Day

Valentine's day, that special time for the year to share with your family, friends and lover and be grateful for everything they've done for you

You might want to share some chocolate with them, then this recipe will help with that

Ingredients:

  • 0.5 cup coconul oil
  • 0.5 cup cocoa powder
  • tablespoons honey
  • 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gently melt coconut oil in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir cocoa powder, honey, and vanilla extract into melted oil until well blended.
  2. Pour the mixture into a candy mold or pliable tray. Refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour.
