Delicious Banana Banana Bread

Want to make a banana bread but don't know how? Then this recipe is for you

This is an easy to follow recipe and you will be able to bake a banana bread for your family

Ingredients:

  • all-purpose flour
  • baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven.
